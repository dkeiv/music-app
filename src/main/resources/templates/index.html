Son Tung M-TP

Sơn Tùng M-TP is a popular Vietnamese singer, songwriter, and music producer known for his unique musical style that blends pop, EDM, and hip-hop. Born in 1994, he gained widespread recognition with hit songs like "Lạc Trôi" and "Hãy Trao Cho Anh". His innovative approach to music, combined with captivating visuals and performances, has made him a significant figure in Vietnam's entertainment industry. Often referred to as a trendsetter, Sơn Tùng M-TP continues to push creative boundaries, earning him millions of fans both domestically and internationally.
